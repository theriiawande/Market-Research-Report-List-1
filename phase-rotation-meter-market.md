<p><ul><li>Rotating Phase Rotation Meter</li><li>Static Phase Rotation Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phase rotation meter market comprises two primary types: rotating phase rotation meters and static phase rotation meters. Rotating phase rotation meters use mechanical components to visually indicate the phase sequence through physical rotation, often providing real-time data. In contrast, static phase rotation meters utilize electronic sensors and digital displays to measure and indicate phase sequence without moving parts, offering enhanced accuracy and reliability. Both types are crucial for electrical applications, ensuring correct phase alignment in three-phase systems.</p></p>
