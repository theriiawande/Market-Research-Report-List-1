<p><p>The three-phase current relays market is primarily divided into under-current and over-current detection types. Under-current detection relays monitor electrical circuits to identify drops below a predefined threshold, protecting against potential motor stalls or failures. Conversely, over-current detection relays safeguard systems by triggering shutdowns or alarms when currents exceed safe limits, preventing equipment damage and ensuring safety. Both types play crucial roles in industrial settings, enhancing operational reliability and minimizing risks associated with electrical malfunctions.</p></p>
