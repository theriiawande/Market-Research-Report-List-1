<p><strong>The Flash Point Testing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cup Flash Point Tester</li><li>Closed Cup Flash Point Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flash Point Testing Equipment market includes two primary types: Open Cup Flash Point Testers and Closed Cup Flash Point Testers. Open Cup testers assess the flash point of liquids by exposing them to the atmosphere, allowing for evaporation; they are typically used for volatile substances. Closed Cup testers, on the other hand, measure flash points in a sealed environment, preventing vapor dispersion and offering more accurate results for a broader range of substances, making them essential for safety evaluations.</p></p>
