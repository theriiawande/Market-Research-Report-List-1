<p><p>Piston espresso machines can be categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require the user to exert pressure to extract espresso, offering complete control over the brewing process. Semi-automatic machines automate the water flow but still require user intervention to grind and tamp the coffee. Fully automatic machines streamline the process by handling grinding, dosing, and brewing with minimal user input, catering to convenience seekers while maintaining quality in espresso preparation.</p></p>
