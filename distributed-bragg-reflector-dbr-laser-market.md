<p><p>The Distributed Bragg Reflector (DBR) laser market comprises two main types: DBR laser diodes and DBR fiber lasers. DBR laser diodes utilize layered semiconductor structures to achieve wavelength-selective feedback, enabling stable emission with high efficiency, making them ideal for telecommunications and sensing applications. Conversely, DBR fiber lasers employ optical fibers with periodic refractive index variations, offering advantages like high output power and excellent beam quality, suitable for industrial manufacturing and medical applications. Both types are vital for modern photonics.</p></p>
