<p><p>The industrial USB connector market encompasses three main types: Industrial USB Type A, Type B, and Type C. Type A connectors are primarily used for standard peripheral connections, offering robust connectivity for devices like keyboards and mice. Type B connectors facilitate communication between devices like printers and PCs, designed for reliability in industrial settings. Type C connectors feature a reversible design and higher data transfer speeds, often integrating power delivery, making them ideal for advanced industrial applications and modern device compatibility.</p></p>
