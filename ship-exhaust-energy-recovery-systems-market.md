<p><strong>The Ship Exhaust Energy Recovery Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Radial</li><li>Single-stage Axial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ship Exhaust Energy Recovery Systems (SEERS) focus on harnessing waste heat from marine engines to enhance efficiency. The market is segmented into two main types: single-stage radial and single-stage axial systems. Single-stage radial systems utilize a centrifugal approach, effectively converting exhaust heat into usable energy through a compact design, ideal for space-constrained vessels. In contrast, single-stage axial systems employ an axial flow mechanism, providing higher efficiency for larger ships, optimizing heat recovery while minimizing energy loss in exhaust gases.</p></p>
