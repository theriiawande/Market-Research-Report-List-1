<p><strong>The Grain Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Grain Dryer</li><li>Mobile Grain Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The grain dryer market comprises primarily two types: stationary and mobile grain dryers. Stationary grain dryers are fixed installations typically found at processing facilities, designed for large-scale drying operations. They offer high capacity and efficiency for commercial grain handling. In contrast, mobile grain dryers are transportable units that provide flexibility for on-farm use, allowing farmers to dry grain directly in the field. Both types cater to different needs, enhancing grain quality and minimizing spoilage during storage.</p></p>
