<p><ul><li>Pelton Turbine</li><li>Turgo Turbine</li><li>Crossflow Turbine</li><li>Francis Turbine</li><li>Kaplan Turbine</li><li>Diagonal Turbine</li><li>Tubular Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pumped Hydroelectric Storage Turbines encompass various types optimized for efficiency and performance. Pelton Turbines are ideal for high-head applications, using a spoon-shaped bucket to harness kinetic energy. Turgo Turbines offer similar benefits but with a more compact design, accommodating medium heads. Crossflow Turbines efficiently utilize water flow across their blades. Francis Turbines are versatile for varying heads and flows, while Kaplan Turbines excel in low-head conditions with adjustable blades. Diagonal and Tubular Turbines serve specialized roles in optimizing flow and energy extraction.</p></p>
