<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Leak Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Reflection</li><li>Penetration</li><li>Resonance</li><li>TOFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic leak testers are categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Pulse Reflection**: This method detects leaks by sending ultrasonic pulses and analyzing reflections from defects or leaks.</p><p>2. **Penetration**: This technique uses high-frequency sound waves to penetrate materials, identifying leaks based on sound transmission.</p><p>3. **Resonance**: This approach measures the resonant frequency changes caused by leaks, enabling precise detection.</p><p>4. **Time of Flight Diffraction (TOFD)**: This method employs sound wave diffraction to locate and size defects, offering detailed analysis of potential leaks.</p></p>
