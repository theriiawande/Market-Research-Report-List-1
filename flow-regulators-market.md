<p><strong>The Flow Regulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volumetric Flow</li><li>Mass Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow regulators market consists of two primary types: volumetric flow and mass flow regulators. Volumetric flow regulators control the flow based on the volume of fluid passing through, ensuring precise flow rates for applications like water and gases. In contrast, mass flow regulators measure and manage the mass of the fluid, providing accuracy for processes that depend on mass rather than volume, such as in the chemical and pharmaceutical industries. Both types cater to specific industry needs for efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
