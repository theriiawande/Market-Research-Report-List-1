<p><p>The manually caulking gun market is primarily divided into two types: closed barrel and open barrel caulking guns. Closed barrel caulking guns feature a sealed cartridge, making them ideal for materials that need to be dispensed without exposure to air, ensuring consistency and reducing wastage. In contrast, open barrel caulking guns accommodate bulk caulking materials, providing flexibility for larger projects but requiring careful handling to prevent material drying. Both types serve essential roles in construction, repairs, and DIY projects.</p></p>
