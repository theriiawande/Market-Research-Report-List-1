<p><p>The mixed current spray drying equipment market includes various atomization technologies tailored for diverse applications. Rotary atomizers utilize centrifugal force for consistent droplet size, ideal for viscous liquids. Nozzle atomizers use pressurized liquid to disperse droplets, suitable for lower viscosity feeds. Fluidized dryers enhance particle uniformity through controlled airflow. Closed-loop systems recycle exhaust for efficiency and reduced emissions. Centrifugal atomizers deliver high-speed atomization for fine particles. Other categories encompass specialized solutions addressing unique drying needs in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
