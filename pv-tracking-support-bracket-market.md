<p><p>PV tracking support brackets are essential components in solar power systems, designed to optimize energy capture by adjusting the orientation of solar panels. There are two primary market types: single-axis and dual-axis trackers. Single-axis trackers pivot on one axis, typically following the sun's east-to-west movement, enhancing efficiency without excessive complexity. In contrast, dual-axis trackers adjust both horizontally and vertically, allowing for maximum sunlight exposure throughout the day and across seasons, resulting in higher energy output but at a greater cost and complexity.</p></p>
