<p><strong>The Rotary Steerable Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push the Bit</li><li>Point the Bit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Steerable Systems (RSS) market can be categorized into two main types: Push the Bit and Point the Bit. </p><p>Push the Bit systems utilize rotary drilling techniques, wherein the bit is actively pushed forward while steering, enabling precise control of the drilling trajectory. In contrast, Point the Bit systems allow for more directional flexibility by orienting the bit without significant forward thrust, making it easier to navigate complex geological formations. Each type serves distinct operational needs in modern drilling applications.</p></p>
