<p><p>The single screw food extruder market is divided into two main types: cold extrusion and hot extrusion. Cold extrusion utilizes lower temperatures to process materials, preserving nutrients and producing products like high-protein snacks and pet foods. Hot extrusion, on the other hand, involves high temperatures and pressure, enabling the processing of starches and creating a variety of snacks, cereals, and pasta. Both methods are essential for maximizing flavor and texture in food products while catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
