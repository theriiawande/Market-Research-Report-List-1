<p><ul><li>Short-wave Length Thermal Camera</li><li>Mid-wave Length Thermal Camera</li><li>Long-wave Length Thermal Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-function thermal camera market consists of three main types based on wavelength: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave thermal cameras. Short-wave length cameras operate in the 0.9 to 3 micrometers range, suitable for capturing images in high-temperature environments. Mid-wave length cameras, ranging from 3 to 5 micrometers, are ideal for detecting heat signatures in various materials. Long-wave length cameras, covering 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in seeing through smoke and fog, making them valuable for security and surveillance applications.</p></p>
